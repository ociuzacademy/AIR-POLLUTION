AirAware - Precautions and Suggestions

Air Pollution Precautions

  • Stay indoors during high pollution hours, especially during early morning and late evening.
  • Wear a mask (N95 or higher) to filter out harmful particles when going outside.
  • Limit physical activities outdoors to reduce exposure to harmful pollutants.
  • Keep windows and doors closed during high pollution days to prevent indoor contamination.
  • Use air purifiers at home to maintain clean air quality indoors.

Suggestions for Reducing Air Pollution

  • Plant trees and other greenery to improve air quality around you.
  • Use public transport, carpool, or bike instead of driving alone to reduce emissions.
  • Switch to electric or hybrid vehicles to lower carbon emissions.
  • Conserve energy by using energy-efficient appliances and turning off lights when not in use.
  • Support policies that aim to reduce industrial pollution and promote cleaner alternatives.
