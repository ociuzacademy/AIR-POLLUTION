The Hidden Dangers of Poor Air Quality

Discover how air pollution impacts your health, mental well-being, and the lives of vulnerable groups like children. Learn the risks and take steps to safeguard your future.

01 How does poor air quality affect your health?

Poor air quality can lead to respiratory issues, allergies, and long-term conditions like asthma and cardiovascular diseases. It can also aggravate existing health problems.

02 Can air pollution impact mental well-being?

Yes, studies show that exposure to polluted air is linked to increased stress, anxiety, and cognitive decline over time, affecting mental health and overall quality of life.

03 Does air pollution affect children differently?

Absolutely. Children are more vulnerable because their lungs are still developing. Prolonged exposure can impact their respiratory growth and increase the risk of illnesses like bronchitis and asthma.
